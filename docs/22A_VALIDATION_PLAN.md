# 14-Day Validation Plan

## Objective

Validate whether TenderFlow solves a real, painful, and paid problem in SME public procurement workflows.

---

## Hypothesis to Validate

1. SMEs struggle to prepare tender responses efficiently
2. Time cost is a major barrier to participation
3. Bid managers would use AI assistance
4. At least 10–20% would be willing to pay

---

## Target Personas (Interviews)

Minimum: 25 interviews

- Bid Managers (primary)
- SME CEOs (secondary decision makers)
- Procurement consultants
- Construction SME operators
- IT consulting SMEs

---

## Interview Questions

- How do you currently prepare tender responses?
- How long does a typical bid take?
- What part is most painful?
- Have you used any software/AI tools?
- What would “10x faster” mean for you?
- Would you pay €100–€300/month for this?
- What would stop you from using it?

---

## Validation Criteria (Pass/Fail)

### PASS conditions:

- ≥5 users say they would pay
- ≥10 confirm pain is “critical”
- ≥3 current tools used but unsatisfied with them
- Clear willingness to test prototype

### FAIL conditions:

- No strong pain signal
- No willingness to pay
- Problem is “nice to have” not critical

---

## Output of this phase

- validated problem statement OR pivot decision
- refined ICP
- refined product scope
