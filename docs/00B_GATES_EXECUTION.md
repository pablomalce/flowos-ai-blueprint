# Execution Gates (Simplified Version)

## Gate 1 — Problem Validation

Must have:
- 25 customer interviews
- clear pain confirmation
- ≥5 willingness-to-pay signals

Status: ❌ NOT STARTED

---

## Gate 2 — Legal Feasibility

Must have:
- EU AI Act classification opinion
- GDPR compliance baseline confirmed
- data handling feasibility validated

Status: ❌ NOT STARTED

---

## Gate 3 — Prototype Validation

Must have:
- working MVP (not production)
- tested with ≥2 real bid managers
- "useful enough to submit a bid" feedback

Status: ❌ NOT STARTED

---

## Gate 4 — Market Validation

Must have:
- at least 3 competitors analyzed
- clear differentiation confirmed
- early traction signals

Status: ❌ NOT STARTED

---

## Gate 5 — Financial Viability

Must have:
- 24-month financial model
- CAC/LTV validated assumptions
- burn rate defined

Status: ❌ NOT STARTED

---

## Rule

No Phase 2 until all gates are either:
- completed OR
- explicitly re-scoped with justification
