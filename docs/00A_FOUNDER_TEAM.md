# Founder & Team Reality

## Founder Context

This project is being developed by a solo founder with strong exposure to:

- Artificial intelligence and LLM-based systems (practical experience building AI-driven workflows and automation systems)
- Data analytics and machine learning concepts (predictive modeling, time-series forecasting, trading systems experimentation)
- Software and automation tooling (building data pipelines, API integrations, and modular systems)
- Product thinking and system design (ability to structure multi-component SaaS platforms)

## Current Strengths

- Strong systems thinking and ability to design complex architectures
- High ability to connect business logic with technical systems
- Experience working with data-driven products and AI workflows
- Strong execution mindset and willingness to iterate fast

## Current Gaps (Critical)

- No direct experience in European public procurement domain
- No enterprise SaaS sales track record
- No prior experience scaling a multi-country SaaS company
- No established network in government / procurement / legal compliance sectors

## How These Gaps Are Being Mitigated

The strategy to compensate for missing domain expertise includes:

1. Early advisory network (procurement/legal experts in Spain/EU)
2. Customer discovery interviews with real bid managers and SMEs
3. Iterative product validation before scaling architecture
4. AI-first product design to reduce dependency on manual expertise
5. Incremental expansion (Spain-first, not multi-country launch)

## Founder Principle

This project is not based on existing domain authority.
It is based on:

- rapid validation
- system design capability
- AI leverage
- iterative learning from real users

If real market validation does not confirm assumptions, the project will be adjusted or stopped.
