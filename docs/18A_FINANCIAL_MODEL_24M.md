# 24-Month Financial Model (Base Case)

## Core Assumptions

- Pricing (ARPA): €250/month average
- Initial target customers: SMBs in Spain
- Sales motion: hybrid PLG + outbound
- Gross margin target: 70% (AI infra costs included)
- Monthly churn: 3–5% early stage
- CAC (blended): €300–€600 per customer

---

## Revenue Ramp (Base Scenario)

### Year 1

- Month 1–3: validation phase → €0 revenue
- Month 4–6: early adopters (5–20 customers)
- Month 7–12: scale to ~100–150 customers

Estimated Year 1 MRR end:
→ €25K–€40K MRR

---

### Year 2

- Expansion of acquisition channels
- Improved conversion from validation learnings
- Introduction of second product module (early cross-sell)

Estimated Year 2:

- 300–600 customers
- €90K–€150K MRR
- €1.1M–€1.8M ARR

---

## Cost Structure

### AI Infrastructure

- LLM API usage: €5–€30 per active customer/month
- Embeddings + retrieval: low marginal cost
- Expected COGS: 20–30% revenue

---

### Fixed Costs

- Cloud infrastructure: €1K–€5K/month early stage
- Legal (EU AI Act + GDPR): €2K–€10K/month averaged
- Initial hires (Year 2): €150K–€300K/year per engineer

---

## CAC Breakdown

- Content/SEO: €150–€250 CAC (long-term)
- Outbound sales: €400–€800 CAC
- Partnerships: variable but efficient at scale

Blended CAC target: €300–€600

---

## Key Risks

- CAC may increase in enterprise segments
- Churn may exceed 5% if product value not immediate
- AI cost volatility (model pricing changes)
- Sales cycle length underestimated in early phases

---

## Reality Check

This is not a validated financial model.
It is a directional framework.

It MUST be updated after:

- 25+ customer interviews
- first working prototype
- first paying users
